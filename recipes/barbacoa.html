Barbacoa

Description

Traditionally a Sunday morning treat, barbacoa is great any time. Barbacoa can be for the more adventurous, but a real treat none-the-less. I do not recmmend substituting the cheek meat with roast; it won't taste the same. Barbacoa has a certain gelatinous texture that is the hallmark of the genre. Serve on corn tortillas with hot sauce, freshly chopped onion, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lb beef cheek meat
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cumin
  • lime
  • white onion
  • cilantro

Steps

  1. cover the beef cheek meat with salt, pepper, and cumin
  2. slice 1/2 an onion
  3. place the seasoned meat and sliced onion in aluminum foil and wrap tightly
  4. place the foil packet in a slow cooker with 1 cup water
  5. cook on low setting for 8-10 hrs
  6. after cooking, remove and shred meat
  7. chop cilantro and the remainder of the onion
  8. cut lime into wedges
  9. serve barbacoa meat on a corn tortilla topped with cilantro, onion, and lime juice
  10. Enjoy!
