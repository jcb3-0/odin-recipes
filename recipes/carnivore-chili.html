Carnivore Chili

Description

I love chili, but it can upset my stomach. This recipe is simple, delicious, and cuts out unecessary ingredients that cause bloating. It's a perfect dish to keep your belly warm on cold nights.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs ground meat (I like a combination of ground beef and pork)
  • 2-3 tbsp chili powder
  • 1-2 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp - 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1/2 - 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • grated cheese, sour cream, full fat greek yogurt (optional)

Steps

  1. Brown the meat in a skillet.
  2. As the meat is cooking, add ingredients: chili powder, cumin, salt, pepper, cayenne, paprika, stirring often.
  3. If desired, add grated cheese, sour cream, or yogurt when serving.
  4. Enjoy!
