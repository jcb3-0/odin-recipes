Steak

Description

Here I will show you how I prepare, cook, and serve a wonderful ribeye steak. I prefer my steaks rare to medium rare (as they should be).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ribeye steak at least 1/2 in thick
  • Coarse salt
  • Fresh ground pepper
  • Butter or Olive oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Remove steak from fridge
  2. Prepare grill so it is piping hot
  3. Coat steak with olive oil or melted butter
  4. Season generously with coarse salt
  5. Season to taste with freshly ground pepper
  6. Grill steak for 3-4 min per side
  7. Enjoy!
